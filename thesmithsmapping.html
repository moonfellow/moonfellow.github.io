just how similar are the smiths songs?

A while ago one of my friends hypothesized that there were two genres of The Smiths songs: a slower, meandering type (think “Please, Please, Please, Let Me Get What I Want”), and a faster, somewhat jolly type with heavier instrumental (think “Frankly, Mr. Shankly). Or in other words, depressing and less depressing. Here's my attempt to test that hypothesis.

track features

energy

danceability

instrumentalness

mapping the similarity of the above three features on a scatterplot

As it turns out, there aren't two distinctive types of The Smiths songs, just a denser region with some outliers ("London," "The Draize Train", etc). Oh well :(
